## 01 — Brand Overview

**Who We Are**

Team Pulsar is a North American esports organisation founded on **April 6th, 2020**. What started as a group of high school friends with a shared dream of competing on the Fortnite main stage has evolved into a multi-title competitive powerhouse with over 60 members, 2 million+ combined reach, and $125,000+ in confirmed prize earnings.

Pulsar operates across three major competitive titles — **Fortnite** (primary), **Apex Legends**, and **Valorant** — with teams performing at consistently high levels across all divisions. The organisation is documented on Liquipedia, the industry-standard esports encyclopedia, solidifying its credibility and competitive track record.

More than just a competitive roster, Team Pulsar is a community. It is a banner that players, content creators, and fans can rally behind — a shared identity built on trust, intent, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Pulsar is not an org people just play for; it's an org people belong to.

## 02 — Mission, Vision & Values

### Mission

> To build an inclusive, high-performance esports community where people bond over gaming, entertainment, and competition — a club people are proud to represent and a part of their identity.

Team Pulsar exists to give gamers, competitors, and creators something bigger than themselves to be a part of. Like a football club, Pulsar offers a shared identity — something to root for, to contribute to, and to carry with pride. The organisation competes at the highest level while nurturing a culture where every member, from ownership to players, operates with purpose and intention.

### Vision

> To become a dominant force in the esports industry within 3–5 years, expanding to 7+ competitive titles, generating consistent profitability, and commanding significant market share in social media and digital exposure across the esports landscape.

### Core Values

**Excellence** — We don't settle. Every player, every piece of content, every decision reflects our commitment to being the best version of ourselves. Excellence is not a destination — it's the standard.

**Community** — Pulsar is more than an org — it's a family. We foster a space where people connect, grow, and share in the wins and losses together. Everyone matters. Everyone is seen.

**Intent** — We don't operate on emotion — we move with purpose. Every action, from recruitment to competition, is deliberate, calculated, and aligned with our mission. Intent separates us from the noise.

## 08 — Visual Identity System

Team Pulsar's visual identity is designed to be sleek, modern, and unmistakably premium. The new brand evolution moves away from the previous purple era into a sophisticated monochromatic palette that communicates professionalism and timeless aesthetics.

### Brand Color Palette

The Pulsar palette is built on a refined grayscale spectrum — from pure black to pure white. This monochromatic system ensures maximum versatility across digital, print, and merchandise applications while maintaining a sleek, high-end feel.

| Hex Code | Usage |
|----------|-------|
| `#000000` | Primary black — deep backgrounds |
| `#0A0A0C` | Dark surface — primary background |
| `#111114` | Dark surface — card backgrounds |
| `#1C1C21` | Dark surface — elevated elements |
| `#505055` | Mid gray — borders, secondary text |
| `#8A8A8F` | Mid gray — subtle accents, metadata |
| `#C8C8CC` | Light gray — body text, descriptions |
| `#E8E8EC` | Light — primary text, headlines |
| `#FFFFFF` | Pure white — CTAs, emphasis |

### Primary Usage
- **#000000 – #1C1C21** — Backgrounds, dark surfaces
- **#FFFFFF – #E8E8EC** — Text, headlines, CTAs
- **#505055 – #8A8A8F** — Secondary text, borders, subtle accents

### Design Principles
- Monochromatic-first approach
- High contrast for readability
- No purple (previous era)
- Accent through typography weight, not colour

### Typography

**Monument Extended** — Primary display typeface. Used for headlines, hero text, and bold statements. Extended width conveys strength and presence. The wide letterforms command attention.

**Bebas Neue** — Secondary display typeface. Used for section headers, navigation, and accent typography. Condensed form provides contrast against Monument Extended while maintaining the bold, clean aesthetic.

### Visual Direction

The new Pulsar visual identity leans into **dark-mode aesthetics**, clean negative space, and bold typography as the primary visual device. Photography and motion graphics should feel **cinematic, high-contrast, and editorial** — never cluttered or overly saturated.

Team Pulsar is entering a new era. The brand evolution represents a complete refresh — new logo, new colours, new visual system, and a renewed commitment to establishing Pulsar as a premium name in esports.

**Year 2 — Expand & Scale** — Expand to 5 competitive titles. Develop structured content pipeline. Launch community programs (amateur tournaments, creator incubator). Begin generating consistent revenue.

**Year 3 — Dominate & Profit** — Reach 7+ competitive titles. Achieve consistent profitability. Hold significant market share in esports social media exposure. Become a household name in competitive gaming.
