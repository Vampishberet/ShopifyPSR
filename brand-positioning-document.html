BRAND OVERVIEW
Who We Are

Team Pulsar is a North American esports organisation founded on April 6th, 2020. What started as a group of high school friends with a shared dream of competing on the Fortnite main stage has evolved into a multi-title competitive powerhouse with over 60 members, 2 million followers, and $125,000+ in confirmed prize earnings.

Pulsar operates across three major competitive titles — Fortnite (primary), Apex Legends, and Valorant — with teams performing at consistently high levels across all divisions. The organisation is documented on Liquipedia, the industry-standard esports encyclopedia, solidifying its credibility and competitive track record.

More than just a competitive roster, Team Pulsar is a community. It is a banner that players, content creators, and fans can rally behind — a shared identity built on trust, intent, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Pulsar is not an org people just play for; it's an org people belong to.

MISSION, VISION & VALUES
What Drives Us

Mission

To build an inclusive, high-performance esports community where people bond over gaming, entertainment, and competition — a club people are proud to represent and a part of their identity.

Team Pulsar exists to give gamers, competitors, and creators something bigger than themselves to be a part of. Like a football club, Pulsar offers a shared identity — something to root for, to contribute to, and to carry with pride. The organisation competes at the highest level while nurturing a culture where every member, from ownership to players, operates with purpose and intention.

Vision

To become a dominant force in the esports industry within 3–5 years, expanding to 7+ competitive titles, generating consistent profitability, and commanding significant market share in social media and digital exposure across the esports landscape.

Core Values

E

EXCELLENCE

We don't settle. Every player, every piece of content, every decision reflects our commitment to being the best version of ourselves. Excellence is not a destination — it's the standard.

C

COMMUNITY

Pulsar is more than an org — it's a family. We foster a space where people connect, grow, and share in the wins and losses together. Everyone matters. Everyone is seen.

I

INTENT

We don't operate on emotion — we move with purpose. Every action, from recruitment to competition, is deliberate, calculated, and aligned with our mission. Intent separates us from the noise.

Key Competitors

A rising org in the competitive Fortnite space. Competes directly with Pulsar for talent and visibility in the same market segment.

An established, legacy esports brand with institutional backing and multi-title history. Represents the benchmark for operational excellence and brand polish in competitive gaming.

GEN.G

A globally respected esports organisation with deep pockets and cross-title presence. Sets the bar for professionalism, content quality, and sponsorship integration.

"We don't work on emotion — we have intent. From the top owners to the players, everyone is on a mission to go above and beyond."

For North American gamers and aspiring esports professionals who want to compete, create, and belong to something greater than themselves, Team Pulsar is the community-driven esports organisation that combines competitive excellence with genuine, intent-driven culture — because unlike organisations that treat players as transactions, Pulsar builds careers, relationships, and legacies.

VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM
How We Look

Team Pulsar's visual identity is designed to be sleek, modern, and unmistakably premium. The new brand evolution moves away from the previous purple era into a sophisticated monochromatic palette that communicates professionalism and timeless aesthetics.

Brand Color Palette

The Pulsar palette is built on a refined grayscale spectrum — from pure black to pure white. This monochromatic system ensures maximum versatility across digital, print, and merchandise applications while maintaining a sleek, high-end feel.

Primary Usage

  • #000000 – #1C1C21 — Backgrounds, dark surfaces
  • #FFFFFF – #E8E8EC — Text, headlines, CTAs
  • #505055 – #8A8A8F — Secondary text, borders, subtle accents

Design Principles

  • Monochromatic-first approach
  • High contrast for readability
  • No purple (previous era)
  • Accent through typography weight, not colour

Typography

MONUMENT EXTENDED

Primary display typeface. Used for headlines, hero text, and bold statements. Extended width conveys strength and presence. The wide letterforms command attention.

BEBAS NEUE

Secondary display typeface. Used for section headers, navigation, and accent typography. Condensed form provides contrast against Monument Extended while maintaining the bold, clean aesthetic.

Visual Direction

The new Pulsar visual identity leans into dark-mode aesthetics, clean negative space, and bold typography as the primary visual device. Photography and motion graphics should feel cinematic, high-contrast, and editorial — never cluttered or overly saturated.
